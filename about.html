The Culinary Pillars

Dum-Cooking Tradition

Slow-cooked over natural charcoal in heavy dough-sealed pots so every grain of rice absorbs the deep marrow and spice aromas.

Daily Fresh Masalas

No pre-packaged pastes. Whole cardamoms, cloves, mace, and Kashmiri chilies are dry roasted and stone-ground fresh every morning.

Home-Style Hospitality

Founded by Chef Surekha with recipes passed down through three generations. Cooked with the same care we serve to our own family.
